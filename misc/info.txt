Action Types:
	Command:	Basic actions, such as Fight, Flee, Defend, etc...
	Magic:		Actions that typically cost MP to use
	Skill:		Actions that cost points from thier own individual pool of points

Magic Types:
	Damage/Restore Stat
	Syphon Stat
	Apply/Remove Status Effect

Status Types:
	Modify Stat Singular
	Modify Stat Per Round

Status Effects:
	Death
	Poison
	Curse
	Regen
